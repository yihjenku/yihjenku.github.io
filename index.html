My Life as a 3rd Year at Columbia

Who am I and how the hell did I wind up here?

Name: Yih-Jen Ku (phonetically, EE-JIN KOO). Although I love my name, this is how I feel about it most of the time.

Hogwarts was my first choice, but I never got my letter. Then I thought I would go with my backup plan of becoming a Pokemon master, but I never got the chance to choose my starter. So, unfortunately, I had to settle for Columbia. Studying Applied Mathematics is still pretty cool I guess.

Even though I'm not a wizard or a Pokemon master, I am in fact a coxswain for Columbia's Varsity Lightweight Rowing team which is magical in its own ways.

As King of the Nerd Herd, I vow to...

1. Make all muggles aware of these wonderful books

3. Keep the fictional feels alive and well. Happy Grading!
